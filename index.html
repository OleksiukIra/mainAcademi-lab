Montenegro Montenegro offers a wide range of tourist services: in summer it is a beach holiday on the Adriatic coast, in winter it is a ski resort. The most popular resorts of Montenegro: Budva Riviera, Sveti Stefan, Petrovac, Ada Bojan, Herceg Novi, Igalo, Budva, Kotor. Popular among foreign tourists are Podgorica, Ulcinj and Cetinje. Many tourists come to Montenegro to visit numerous monasteries and churches, rafting in the mountain canyons.
Italy According to experts, Italy is a tourist product of the highest degree of perfection: international statistics give it an honorable place in the five most developed in terms of tourism in the countries of the world, since that country accounts for 6% of all tourist "turnover" of our planet. Italy's world-famous history, the beauty of its nature, works of art, cultural monuments, religious holidays, fashion and Italian lifestyles, wine and gastronomy are the key points that have made Italy an important world-class tourist destination. 12 of the Italian GDP forms the tourism services sector, which is thus one of the main industries of the national industry.
Norway Norway has a well-developed road network. At gas stations there are only two types of fuel: gasoline (one type) and diesel fuel. The overall speed limit on highways outside the city is 80 km / h, and in settlements 50 km / h.
